Customer Focus

Products

A curated range of structural and safety glass solutions for every project.

Toughened / Tempered Glass

High-impact resistance and safety when broken. Standard sizes or custom-cut to project specifications.

Laminated Safety Glass

Multi-layer designs with PVB or SGP interlayers for enhanced security and acoustic control.

Insulated Glass Units (IGU)

High-performance double or triple glazed units with Low-E coatings for thermal efficiency.
